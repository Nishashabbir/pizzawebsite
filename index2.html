OUR SERVICES

At BITCOIN, we strive to provide exceptional service to ensure your dining experience is unforgettable. From dining in to special events, our array of services is designed to cater to your every need.

Dine-In Experience

Enjoy a cozy and inviting atmosphere at BITCOIN. Our beautifully designed interior offers comfortable seating for individuals, couples, and groups. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal, our friendly staff is here to provide top-notch service.

Takeout and Delivery

Craving our delicious pizza but prefer to enjoy it at home? We offer convenient takeout and delivery services. Order online through our website or your favorite delivery apps such as bit-food, and have our piping hot pizzas delivered straight to your door with contactless delivery options for your safety.

Private Events and Reservations

Looking for a venue to host a private event? BITCOIN offers exclusive use of our dining area or private rooms for special occasions. Our dedicated event coordinator will assist you in planning every detail, from customized menus to décor, to make your event seamless and special. Contact us to reserve your date.

Special Services

Cooking Classes: Learn the art of pizza-making with our hands-on cooking classes. Perfect for team-building activities or a fun outing with friends. Gift Cards: Share the love of great food with BITCOIN gift cards, available for any occasion. Seasonal Offerings: Check out our seasonal specials and limited-time offers, crafted to highlight the best ingredients of the season.

Welcome to BitcoinPizza , where every slice tells a story! Nestled in the heart of MULTAN, our pizzeria is more than just a place to eat—it's a community hub where friends and family come together to share a love for authentic, Delicious pizza

History and Backgrround

Our journey began in 2023 when Mr.FAISAL SHEHZAD decided to bring a piece of Italy to MULTAN. With a passion for traditional recipes and a dream of creating the perfect pizza, MR.FAISAL opened the doors of joy. Since then, we’ve been serving up slices of happiness to our beloved community.

UNIQUE SELLING POINTS

At Bitcoin, we believe in the magic of fresh ingredients and authentic flavors. Our dough is made from scratch daily, our sauce simmers to perfection with a blend of secret herbs and spices, and our toppings are always fresh and locally sourced. We bake our pizzas in a traditional wood-fired oven, ensuring a crispy crust with a delightful smoky flavor in every bite.

VALUES & PHILOSOPHY

We are committed to quality, community, and sustainability. Our team works tirelessly to ensure every pizza meets our high standards of excellence. We also believe in giving back to the community that has supported us over the years, whether through local partnerships or by participating in community events. At BITCOIN , we’re not just making pizza—we’re making memories.

OUR TEAM

Our team is a family, and we extend that warmth to everyone who walks through our doors. From our skilled pizzaiolos to our friendly waitstaff, each member of our team is passionate about delivering a top-notch dining experience. We take pride in fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment for both our staff and our guests.

INVITATION

We invite you to join us at Bitcoin and experience the love and care we put into every pizza. Whether you’re here for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, we promise to make your visit memorable. Come and taste the difference that passion makes!
